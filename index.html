RPG: The Board Game

Workshop Content for Tabletop Simulator

A silly and absurd board game for 2+ players that parodies anything and everything that can be considered a role playing game-- as well as a bunch of other unrelated media. It's a blast to play with friends, and the ridiculous amount of content makes no session the same. Oh-- and you get to kill God at the end!


I designed this game in its entirety while I was sick over the winter break of 2024. The original concept came to me in a fever dream, and after a while's work of deciphering my feverish sketches, I went right to making the game and playtesting it with my friends. At the time, I had no idea what went into making a board game. I was purely riding on design and a dream, which at least led to a pretty fun game to play with my friends.


One year later (which was coincidentally the year I took a tabletop game design class in college), I returned to the project with a fresh perspective and new ideas and create the Green Update, for which I reworked just about everything, and added a ton of new stuff (including of course the color green.)

Afterlife After Hours

Animated Webseries

Welcome to Afterlife After Hours, the only show in Limbo! Afterlife After Hours is a webseries featuring a fictional news show for the troubled souls of Limbo. Join Skulson and his friends as they deliver the news of an empty void and experience emotional drama, while they struggle against the trauma that keeps them from moving on!


Starting in 2023 and still ongoing across seven released episodes, Afterlife After Hours is my biggest passion project. The show is animated in After Effects using assets drawn in Procreate and utilizes skills across Premiere Pro for audio editing and Audacity for audio recording and mastery, all of which is done entirely by myself. Of course, I'm especially thankful to have my friends around to play the additional members of the cast.


I've been keeping a blog of the show's development over on Tumblr. I would've put some posts in the Gallery section, but it was getting pretty laggy with all the other posts there too. So instead, you can go right to the blog by clicking on this card!

QUEST.

Video Game for the Playdate

Enter a cryptic world with little left to give and embark on the quest it has set for you. Connect with the remains of inhabitants in order to bring about a conclusion. Enough effort may bring about a better end for everyone.


QUEST. is a short, narrative-driven adventure game for the Playdate handheld, developed entirely by myself using the Playdate's Pulp web editor. Created in late-2025 to early-2026, right after my graduation in December, which is when I got a Playdate for Christmas! It's a wonderful little device, and PulpScript is such simple fun to work with!
